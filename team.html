Joseph Ayoub

CEO

Joseph is a Warwick graduate, having worked with Lazard, Arabesque on the AI team, and within Global Markets at Citibank. Joseph is the Co-founder of Crypcentra, a digital asset data solution platforms, featured in the Financial Times. He’s worked exclusively in the digital asset market as a thought leader for the last five years, developing and delivering courses to over 10,000 people globally, supported by substantial digital asset trading experience.

Igor Cardoso

Director of Quantsr

Igor is a university of Brasilia graduate in electrical engineering. Having worked with Cisco, Citrix and notably the Bank of Brazil, Igor has extensive knowledge in both high-level programming and data center networks. Coupled with experience developing programs within financial institutions, Igor is currently focusing his doctoral research around disruptive technology, data analysis and statistical inference.

Diogo Morgado

CTO

Diogo is a UFMG graduate in Computer Sciences and neuroscience. As a high-level programmer, Diogo has worked with HSMs, UTMs and next gen firewalls. With 10 years of Crypto-currency Diogo was heavily involved in Bitcoin’s infancy, mining Major-Caps in 2010. Diogo is now focusing his research around Machine Learning, Artificial intelligence and their applicability within the Blockchain space.

Toni Fola Alade

Director of Capital formation

Toni is a First Class graduate of the University of Cambridge, having worked at Goldman Sachs Asset Management. Toni is the Co-Founder of Nomad integrated, building digital banking solutions for the world’s biggest banks. He now acts as an advisor to a number of digital asset companies, working closely with family offices to build tailored digital investment solutions.
