Empros Fund

Our Empros Fund is currently in development. By Q4 in 2021 we aim to have an FCA regulated fund facilitating investment to an institutional standard.

The Empros Fund will incorporate all 4 strategies listed on the strategies page. Each strategy will be weighted using artificial intelligence to determine the optimum weighting. The funds main aim is to generate risk adjusted returns that are meticulously centred around risk management whilst safely maintaining exposure to the underlying digital asset market.
