Empros Systematic

Background

Empros systematic is built to generate market alpha via artificial intelligence to achieve risk adjusted returns. Alpha is a measure of active return on an investment in comparison to the risk free rate. Each market is different, and our alpha is calculated using Bitcoin as the risk-free rate, based on our notion that bitcoin is, in and of itself, the market index.


Our systematic strategy, automatically executes buy and sell signals on a multiplicity of timeframes, operating on a number of different asset classes. Harnessing the power of AI, our traders accurately predict and trade patterns, generating significant market index outperformance.




Evidence

Our systematic traders are built using Artificial intelligence that predicts returns via a multiple of data sets, notoriously difficult due to the markets non-linear financial time series. As evidenced by Qiu (2016), Artificially intelligent models can generate market predictions via classical ‘Back Propagation’ learning algorithms (BP).

Using predictive models, Artificial intelligence can accurately predict market performance using Empros’ uniquely developed datasets that underpin market behaviour. On top of this, Wavelet models, as researched by Zhao (2008), can work in harmony with BP models to eliminate simulation error, and deliver more accurate results.

Artificial intelligence lacks emotion, and can analyse data infinitely more efficiently than a human, as well as act upon that data through systematic trading. Below is one of many examples of the consistent out-performance of artificially intelligent models compared to discretionary funds.
