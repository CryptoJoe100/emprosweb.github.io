Join Empros


The Empros team is growing rapidly. We are looking for individuals with an entrepreneurial spirit, and a passion for digital assets to help us keep building, and creating the new industry standard.


Talented individuals, with a passion for digital assets, Blockchain and decentralisation can submit their application by emailing us. We are currently recruiting for the following roles:


Developers

As highlighted by Avellaneda (2008) with his study on Stat Arb in the US equities market, mean reversion processes can generate low-risk signals. Due to its fundamentally low-risk nature, accounting for transaction costs and signal speed, Stat Arb generates notably high risk adjusted returns.



Traders

Traders should be comfortable building quantitative models, with strong financial experience. Traders will help create existing models, and have a curiosity to discover and develop further market opportunities.



Security

We’re looking for an experienced IT security developer comfortable working on a range of security aspects.



Operations

Our operations team works on the tax, compliance and relationship management side of the business. Building strategic relationships, working on tax models, and working with regulators in a nascent space is a crucial part of our business. Digital asset experience is helpful but not necessary; we’re looking for pro-activity with an eagerness to learn.



DevOps

Empros is building a number of fully integrated, high performing products, tailor built for our clients with a multitude of optionality. We’re looking for SRE engineers who are comfortable setting up CI and systems on an AWS infrastructure.
